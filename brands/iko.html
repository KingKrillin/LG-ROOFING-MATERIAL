WHO IS IKO

IKO is a family-owned, global manufacturer known for combining durable construction with strong curb appeal across its shingle lineup.

Lines like Cambridge and Dynasty are popular for their dimensional profile and broad color selection, making IKO a solid fit for a wide range of home styles.
